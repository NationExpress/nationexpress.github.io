India Launches New AI Innovation Mission

Government announces nationwide AI development initiative.

By Raj Sharma
|
Published: 30-06-2026

The Government of India has officially launched a national AI innovation mission aimed at boosting research and technology development.

The initiative focuses on providing financial support to startups, universities, and research institutions working in artificial intelligence.

Officials stated that the mission will encourage innovation while promoting responsible and ethical use of AI technologies.

Several leading technology companies have expressed interest in collaborating with educational institutions under the new program.

The government also plans to establish AI research centers across multiple states over the next few years.

Experts believe the initiative could create thousands of new jobs in software development, robotics, healthcare, and automation.

Students will benefit from specialized AI training courses and scholarships introduced under the program.

Industry analysts say India has significant potential to become one of the world's leading AI development hubs.

Small businesses are expected to receive access to affordable AI tools through government-supported digital platforms.

The first phase of the mission will begin later this year with pilot projects in education and healthcare sectors.
